Shrimp Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 200g of pre-made frozen small shrimps
  • 500g of boiled rice
  • 200g of frozen vegetables mix
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 50g green onion
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Boil 400ml of water in a small saucepan.Once water is boiling add the rice and boil for 12-15min
  2. Heat the pan and put on some Olive oil. Empty the full back of the shrimps on to the pan.
  3. Add rice once cooked and frozen vegetables. Mix for 1 min and then add two full eggs.
  4. Mix on low heat for 2-5min after that the dish is ready to be served
